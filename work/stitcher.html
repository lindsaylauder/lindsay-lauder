stitcher pitch

Podcasts have become increasingly popular as the smartphone industry continues to grow rapidly. However, the technology that supports the creation and distribution of podcasts has evolved at a much slower pace compared to other mediums. Stitcher is one of the most widely used podcast platforms on the market right now.

This is a pitch for a feature designed to improve the overall Stitcher experience. In particular, it was designed to test the assumption that listeners who interact with other users have a better overall experience. The pitch covers the problem, potential alternatives as well as an overview of the feature chosen.

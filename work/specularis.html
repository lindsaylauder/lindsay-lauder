specularis product design

This collaborative project involved designing a new device or creating a piece of interactive media "that extended the human body or mind." My group created Specularis - an innovative healthcare product intended to streamline the diagnostic process through realtime imaging.

Our design aimed to strike a balance between the new and the familiar. We wanted a look that was fresh, bright, and intriguing, but one that would instill a sense of comfort and reliability, not threatening to either doctor or patient.
